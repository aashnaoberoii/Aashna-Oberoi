What I Do

BRANDING
I specialize in creating cohesive brand identities that resonate with audiences. Notable projects include the Re-branding of Miam Bakery, Taj Baristaire Gurgaon, Taj 24 Alive and Self Branding Project
UI UX DESIGN
With experience in both website and app design, I’ve worked on the Jigyasa a learning app, Vogue app redesign, websites for Craft Cluster and ANI Clothing, and revamped the HUFT app for a more seamless user experience.
WEBSITE DESIGN
I design engaging and functional websites tailored to user needs. Projects include redesigning the ANI Clothing website, creating a site for Craft Cluster, and developing this portfolio website. My approach integrates visual appeal with intuitive navigation for a seamless user experience.
GRAPHIC DESIGN
I’ve designed impactful visuals for print and digital platforms, including Lutopia Magazine and Charlette Magazine, ensuring a blend of creative storytelling and strategic communication across various media.
VISUAL COMMUNICATION
From print to digital, I craft clear, compelling visuals. My work on the NIFT publication and ANI Clothing website redesign focuses on delivering effective communication through thoughtful design and layout.
STYLING & ART DIRECTION
I’ve worked on styling and art direction for projects such as the Zomato calendar, Grandapacore and multiple group projects showcased on Behance, drawing inspiration from diverse sources like family and cultural icons.
